Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where is my data stored? In your private iCloud (Apple CloudKit). MountainBucks has no servers and cannot read your data.
  • Does it work offline? Yes. You can add and review everything offline; changes sync through iCloud the next time you're online.
  • I changed device — is my data still there? Yes, as long as you sign in with the same Apple ID and iCloud is enabled for MountainBucks.
  • How do shared expenses and Family work? Through Apple's iCloud sharing: only the people you invite can see the shared group, and the data never leaves iCloud.
  • How do I manage or cancel my subscription? From your Apple ID settings on your device (Settings → your name → Subscriptions).
